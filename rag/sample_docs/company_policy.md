# Company Policies

## Remote Work Policy

Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 3 days per week. Remote work must be approved by your direct manager at least 24 hours in advance. All employees must be available during core hours (10 AM - 3 PM EST) regardless of location.

## Leave Policy

Full-time employees receive 20 days of paid time off (PTO) per year. PTO accrues monthly and can be carried over up to 5 days to the following year. Sick leave is unlimited but requires a doctor's note for absences longer than 3 consecutive days.

## Equipment Reimbursement

The company provides a $1,500 annual stipend for home office equipment. This can be used for monitors, keyboards, chairs, or desk accessories. Requests must be submitted through the finance portal with receipts.

## Professional Development

Each employee has access to a $2,000 annual learning budget. This can be used for courses, conferences, certifications, or books related to professional development. Budget resets every fiscal year and does not roll over.
