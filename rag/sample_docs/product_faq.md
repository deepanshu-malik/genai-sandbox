# Product FAQ

## Pricing

### What are your pricing tiers?

We offer three pricing tiers:
- Starter: $29/month for up to 1,000 API calls
- Professional: $99/month for up to 10,000 API calls
- Enterprise: Custom pricing for unlimited calls with SLA guarantees

Annual subscriptions receive a 20% discount.

### Is there a free trial?

Yes! All new users get a 14-day free trial with access to all Professional tier features. No credit card required to start.

## Features

### What API rate limits apply?

Rate limits depend on your tier:
- Starter: 10 requests per second
- Professional: 100 requests per second
- Enterprise: Custom limits based on agreement

### Do you support webhooks?

Yes, webhooks are available on Professional and Enterprise tiers. You can configure up to 10 webhook endpoints per account.

## Refunds

### What is your refund policy?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee on all plans. If you're not satisfied, contact support for a full refund within 30 days of purchase. No questions asked.
